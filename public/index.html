About Bukuma Radio 📻

Bukuma Radio is your community's premier internet radio station, dedicated to celebrating culture, music, and community through the power of sound.

We specialize in bringing you the finest Highlife music from legends like Cardinal Rex Lawson, alongside community news, cultural programming, and more.

  • Live 24/7 streaming music

  • DJ-controlled live sessions
  • Cultural programming & events
