About Agum Bukuma Community Radio 📻

The core purpose of our station is to deliver vital news, drive community initiatives, and ultimately foster unity among our people.

Through our broadcasts—including the legendary sounds of Highlife—we are dedicated to sharing the rich stories and giving the world a true taste of our vibrant riverine community.
